Pizza Theory

Ingredients

    25 grams Fresh Yeast
    200 millilitrers of water
    2 tablespoons of olive oil
    200 grams of strength flour
    1 teaspoon of fine salt
    100 grams tomato sauce
    80 grams mozzarella cheese
    80 grams goat cheese
    60 grams smoke bacon
    50 grams caramelized onion

Time

    The processing time,
    dough rest and bake,
    is approximately 65 minutes.

Preparation

    Mix 25 grams of fresh yeast with two and a half cups of warm water ( about 200 ml).
    Add two tablesponons of olive oil
    Add 200 grams of flour
    Knead well until the dough stops sticking to your fingers
    Let the dough rest for 40-45 minutes
    Give the dough a round shape to add the ingredents on it
    Add the crushed tomato sauce first until you can cover its surface
    Add the caramelized onion, about 50 grams, spread over the surface
    Add 80 grams of diced mozzarella cheese, spread over the surface
    Add 80 grams of crumbled goat cheese and spread over the surface
    Add 60 grams of bacon in cubes spread over the surface
